# Imagination & AI Art Competition — Starts 24.04!
## [Topic]: The Origin of the World
Think of your own theory that explains the existence of the world, and visualize it using generative AI.  
Your submission must include:  
- a text file describing your theory (recommended around 100 words)
- an AI-generated image that illustrates it
## [Rules]
Your work should attempt to answer fundamental questions such as:  
Why does the world exist? How did it begin? What is the reason, logic, or purpose behind it? Is it part of something bigger? What does it all look like?

The main requirement is that your theory is **consistent** and has a **detailed visual representation**. The image and the text must have clear connection.

*If 1 image is not enough to illustrate your theory, you may submit up to 3 images, provided they depict the same theory from different angles.*

**Proof of genuinity**: add screenshots with some of your prompts, that you used to generate images.
